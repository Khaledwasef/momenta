About Momenta

Our Mission

To create transformative moments that build momentum for students' personal and professional growth, fostering a supportive community where every member can thrive.

Our Vision

To be the catalyst for student success, creating impactful experiences that build lifelong connections and momentum for future achievements.

"At Momenta, we believe that small moments can create big momentum. Join us and be part of something transformative."

Our Founding Members

Meet the visionary students who laid the foundation for Momenta and transformed an idea into a thriving community.

Founder

Co-Founder & Visionary

Sarah conceived the idea of Momenta during her sophomore year. Her passion for creating meaningful student experiences drove the club's initial formation.

Founder

Founder

Emily Chen

Our Founding Story

Spring 2019

The idea for Momenta was born during a campus leadership conference where the founders first connected.

Fall 2019

Official club proposal submitted and approved by the Student Activities Office.

January 2020

First general meeting held with 35 founding members in attendance.

March 2020

Successfully organized the first large-scale campus event with over 200 participants.

Present

Momenta has grown to over 500 active members and continues to create impactful student experiences.
