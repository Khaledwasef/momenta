Our Founding Story

2019

Momenta Family was founded by Dr. Tamer AbouElnaga, starting as a small student initiative inside the institute.

2020

The family organized its first events and activities that attracted dozens of students, building a strong community spirit.

2021

Momenta Family grew significantly and became a well-known student organization within the institute.

Present

Now, Momenta Family is supervised by Dr. Osama Tharout, continuing to empower students and expand its impact.
